# Door Triggers & Parent-Program Reference (Researcher)

## The three green-light triggers (any fires -> GREEN)
1. ONBOARDING LANGUAGE — page text: "carrier onboarding", "carrier setup",
   "become a carrier", "carrier registration", "haul for us", "carrier requirements",
   a transportation/carrier page inviting carriers.
2. OWN FREIGHT OPERATION — captive logistics arm, in-house transport dept, named
   logistics subsidiary, OR captive FMCSA carrier/broker authority (check SAFER/DOT#).
3. LIVE SIGNUP PORTAL — proprietary portal, or named platform: RMIS, MyCarrierPackets,
   Highway, TRUX, KBX, or similar.

## EXCLUDE/FLAG checklist
- Defunct? (verify still operating)
- NAICS mismatch? (verify what they actually make)
- Name collision? (verify DOT#/address — Trinity/Tejas/Atlas Pallet/Amega/Texas Metal Fab)
- Captive-fleet-only? (tiny intrastate 1-2 truck DOT = contact-lead, not open door)
- Wrong trailer? (route to other-vertical bin)
