# Privacy Policy — A2P fixes to apply at acedispatch.us/privacy-policy
Your current Termify policy fails A2P for two reasons: (1) it says data is shared with "advertisers, contest sponsors, promotional and marketing partners," and (2) it has no SMS section. Make these changes.

## Change 1 — REPLACE the entire "Do we share the information we collect with third parties?" section with:
> We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share your personal information with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or advertising purposes. We share personal information only with service providers who perform functions on our behalf (for example, our telephone/SMS provider, our CRM, and our payment processor), and only as needed to provide our services to you; in connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or sale of assets); or when required by law or to protect our legal rights. We may share aggregated or anonymized, non-personal data for analytics.
>
> Mobile information (phone numbers and SMS/text-messaging consent) is never shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

Also delete any remaining lines mentioning sharing with advertisers, promotional/marketing partners, or sharing log-file data with third parties.

## Change 2 — ADD a new section (place it right after "What Information Do We Collect?"):
> **SMS / Text Messaging Terms & Consent**
> When you provide your mobile number to Ace Dispatch — by giving it to one of our representatives during a call, by texting us first, or by submitting it through a form on acedispatch.us and agreeing to be contacted — you consent to receive text messages from us about your inquiry, your account, quotes, dispatching and load coordination, scheduling, and customer support.
> - Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.
> - Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help, or contact admin@acedispatch.us.
> - Your mobile opt-in information and consent are never shared with third parties for marketing.
> - Wireless carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
