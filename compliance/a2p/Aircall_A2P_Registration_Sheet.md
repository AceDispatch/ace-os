### Campaign description (paste verbatim)
> Ace Dispatch sends conversational, account, and customer-care text messages to motor carriers who have contacted us, who have spoken with our representatives by phone and verbally agreed to receive texts, or who have opted in through a consent checkbox on acedispatch.us. Messages include follow-ups to inquiries, freight rate quotes, carrier onboarding and paperwork, dispatch and load-coordination updates, scheduling, and support replies. We do not purchase or text cold contact lists and we do not send unsolicited marketing.

### How subscribers opt in — "Call-to-Action" (paste verbatim)
> Carriers provide express consent to receive texts in one of three ways: (1) verbally, during a phone call with an Ace Dispatch representative, who logs the consent (date and method) in our CRM; (2) by texting our number first to begin a conversation; (3) by submitting a contact form on acedispatch.us that includes a clearly labeled, unchecked-by-default SMS consent checkbox. The opt-in language, Privacy Policy, and Terms are published on acedispatch.us. Mobile opt-in data and consent are never shared with third parties for marketing.

> If the form lets you attach a file, upload a **screenshot of the website consent checkbox** here — it materially improves approval odds.

### Sample messages (paste — these cover the "mixed" content types)
1. Hi {first_name}, this is {rep} with Ace Dispatch following up on our call. Here's the rate we discussed for your {lane}: {rate}. Want me to book it? Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.
2. Ace Dispatch: your carrier packet is approved — you're all set for dispatch. Reply here with any questions. Reply STOP to opt out.
3. Ace Dispatch: load update — pickup confirmed for {date} at {city}. Your rate con is in your email. Reply STOP to opt out.
4. Hi {first_name}, Ace Dispatch here — we have your MC paperwork; we just need your signed W-9 to finish onboarding. Reply STOP to opt out.

### Auto-replies & keywords
- **Opt-in confirmation:** "Ace Dispatch: you're confirmed to receive texts about your account and inquiries. Msg frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help, STOP to opt out."
- **HELP reply:** "Ace Dispatch: support at admin@acedispatch.us or 561-231-2023. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe."
- **STOP reply:** "You're unsubscribed from Ace Dispatch and will receive no further texts. Reply START to rejoin."
- **Keywords:** Opt-out = STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT · Help = HELP, INFO · Opt-in = START, UNSTOP
