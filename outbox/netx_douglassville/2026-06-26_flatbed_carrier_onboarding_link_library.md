## Standouts to action (flatbed, near Douglassville)
- **Tenaris ⭐** — `vendorsportal-usa.apps.tenaris.com` — Maverick Tube **Blytheville AR** + Tenaris **Hickman AR** (OCTG/line pipe). A real self-serve carrier portal right in the golden-goose steel cluster — pairs with Nucor for the steel reload back from the Memphis/Blytheville end.
- **Steel Dynamics ⭐** — `steeldynamics.com/transportation/` — Marshall Steel (TN), another major steel carrier program.
- **Hybar ⭐** — `hybarsteel.com/contact/` — brand-new Osceola AR rebar mill (started 2024) near Blytheville — early-mover door.
- **Oldcastle/CRH** — `oldcastlelogistics.com/carrier-setup` — national precast/aggregates program with *published* insurance + 14-straps/load flatbed rule; CRH has plants region-wide (worth checking for a NE-TX/Shreveport site).

> Insurance floor to clear all of these: **$1M auto · $100K cargo (steel wants $200–250K) · $1M/$2M GL · statutory WC**, plus naming the shipper/3PL additional insured + waiver of subrogation.
