## The insurance floor — carry this to qualify almost anywhere

Most big shippers **do not publish** carrier minimums (they hand them over in the setup packet). But the two that DO publish (Nucor, KBX) plus the industry standard converge on the same floor. Have the agent issue a COI for:

| Coverage | Floor to carry | Notes |
|----------|----------------|-------|
| **Auto / Trucking Liability** | **$1,000,000** | Universal floor |
| **Cargo** | **$100,000** | Steel / high-value often wants **$200K–$250K** (Nucor = $200K for HVL) |
| **General Liability** | **$1,000,000 / occ** ($2M aggregate) | |
| **Workers' Comp** | **Statutory** + Employers' Liability | |
| **Endorsements** | Name the shipper/3PL **additional insured**, policy **primary**, **waiver of subrogation** | KBX & Nucor require these explicitly |

Exact figures are confirmed per-shipper at setup — but a carrier carrying the above clears the bar at essentially every door on this sheet.
